About us

Original Japanese Restaurant

As the first Japanese restaurant in Cardiff, we’ve built and proudly maintained our reputation as one of the best restaurants in the city.
Since we first opened our doors in 1999, Tenkaichi has been serving quality, authentic Japanese cuisine to happy customers across Cardiff. From our bustling Sushi and Noodle Bar on City Road, we offer a unique in-house dining experience, as well as takeaway and home delivery options.

Fresh, Authentic Japanese Food

Renowned for our authentic Japanese food, great atmosphere and excellent customer service, we’re here to give you the ultimate taste of Japan! From the freshest, sustainably sourced sashimi and sushi to hearty rice and noodle dishes, our menu has something for everyone. So whether you’re looking for the perfect venue for a first date or family party, or just want to treat yourself to a healthy Japanese takeaway, simply book a table at our City Road restaurant or place your takeaway or home delivery order online.
